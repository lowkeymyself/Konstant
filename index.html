What is Konstant

Konstant is a precision universal bike script for Roblox — built first for supermoto and ebike games, now expanding to cover bike, scooter, and movement-based games generally. It ships as a single loadstring with a full cheat and physics suite, minimal interface, and zero bloat.

Konstant — Universal Bike Script

Getting started

You need a script executor that supports hookmetamethod, writefile, and fireproximityprompt. Most modern executors work fine.

Copy the loadstring below after logging in. Paste it into your executor while in the game. The menu shows up automatically. If you already ran it before, toggle it or use the deduplication guard at the top, it cleans itself up on reload.

Tabs overview

The main menu has five tabs. Money handles the economy exploits. Bikes gives you speed, brake, turn patches plus a bunch of physics mods. Mods is where the fun stuff lives: fly mode, anti-fall, rainbow, speed underglow, wheelie and stoppie controls, drift stuff. Troll handles fling logic. ESP gives you overlays on other players and bikes.

Settings saves your config to disk through LinoriaLib, so your toggles carry over between sessions.

Bike patching

Get on your bike first, then hit Patch. Speed and acceleration are in mph, brake force is in mph/s, turn speed in degrees per second. They all work independently. Unpatching any of them disconnects the heartbeat loop for that specific one.

Auto-fix Bike handles the back wheel clipping issue. When the game tweens the wheel to your click position on mount, it catches it, locks the wheel back to its pre-mount offset relative to the seat, and cycles gear state until it reads neutral.

Auto-land detects when you're 2–8 studs above the ground and gradually corrects your roll and pitch back to upright using a CFrame lerp. It preserves your yaw and never touches velocity, so it doesn't interfere with actual speed. Nitro Burst fires a forward acceleration burst on a configurable key with a cooldown and a HUD bar that shows charge state.

Configs

Bike customization configs are saved to Konstant/bike_appearance_configs.json in your executor's workspace folder. They survive reloads. If the file gets corrupted it gets wiped clean on next load so it doesn't stay broken.

The LinoriaLib config for your toggle states autosaves and loads with a small delay on startup so it doesn't interfere with game initialization.

Notes on testing builds

Testing builds pull from a separate branch and may have partially finished features or logic that changes between sessions. They work, but they're not stable. If something breaks in testing, that's expected.

Konstant A* — Waypoint Autodriver

What is Konstant A*

Konstant A* is a universal waypoint autodriver. You record a route once by simply driving it — the script samples your line every stud and paints it onto the ground as you go. Made a mistake? Hold rewind and the path walks you backwards so you can redo the section: green is your path, red is the part you're replacing, yellow is the redone line. End the path at your destination, name it, and it saves per-game to your executor's workspace.

On playback it doesn't teleport or glide — it actually drives. A pure pursuit controller steers the vehicle along your recorded line using the game's own physics, follows your recorded speed profile, brakes for anything blocking the lane, and resumes when it clears. Each run it learns: corners it took too wide get braked earlier next time.

Universal Pure Pursuit Per-Game Saves

Using it

Run the loadstring in any game, click the K icon at the top of your screen, and hit Start Path. The UI minimizes into a compact HUD while you drive. When you're done, End Path opens the save dialog. Your saved routes live in KonstantAStar/<gameId>/ in your executor workspace and only appear inside the game they were recorded in.

To drive a saved route, sit in any vehicle near the path, open the Load tab, pick the route, and hit Start Drive. The HUD shows progress, speed, ETA, and what it's waiting on if something blocks the road. If a game's chassis ignores seat control, it falls back to simulated key input automatically.
