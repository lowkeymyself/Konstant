What is Konstant

Konstant is an ongoing series of high-quality, client-side Lua scripts for Roblox. We focus on building the best, most polished execution suites with minimal interfaces and absolute precision.

Our current flagship release is built exclusively for Roblox supermoto and ebike games. What started as a small physics experiment has grown into a massive toolkit covering everything from advanced bike physics and custom tuning, to map loading, ESP, and economy exploits.

Lua 5.1 Roblox LinoriaLib Client-side

Getting started

You need a script executor that supports hookmetamethod, writefile, and fireproximityprompt. Most modern executors work fine.

Copy the loadstring below after logging in. Paste it into your executor while in the game. The menu shows up automatically. If you already ran it before, toggle it or use the deduplication guard at the top, it cleans itself up on reload.

Tabs overview

The main menu has five tabs. Money handles the economy exploits. Bikes gives you speed, brake, turn patches plus a bunch of physics mods. Mods is where the fun stuff lives: fly mode, anti-fall, rainbow, trail, wheelie and stoppie controls, drift stuff. Troll handles fling logic. ESP gives you overlays on other players and bikes.

Settings saves your config to disk through LinoriaLib, so your toggles carry over between sessions.

Bike patching

Get on your bike first, then hit Patch. Speed and acceleration are in mph, brake force is in mph/s, turn speed in degrees per second. They all work independently. Unpatching any of them disconnects the heartbeat loop for that specific one.

Auto-fix Bike handles the back wheel clipping issue. When the game tweens the wheel to your click position on mount, it catches it, locks the wheel back to its pre-mount offset relative to the seat, and cycles gear state until it reads neutral.

Configs

Bike customization configs are saved to Konstant/bike_appearance_configs.json in your executor's workspace folder. They survive reloads. If the file gets corrupted it gets wiped clean on next load so it doesn't stay broken.

The LinoriaLib config for your toggle states autosaves and loads with a small delay on startup so it doesn't interfere with game initialization.

Notes on testing builds

Testing builds pull from a separate branch and may have partially finished features or logic that changes between sessions. They work, but they're not stable. If something breaks in testing, that's expected.
